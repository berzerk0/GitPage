Content Reflector

This document will use document.write to append the contents of a URL parameter to the body.
Place content intothe URL's ?content= parameter, then reload the page

Example:

reflector.html?content=<script>alert(1)</script>


Beware:
This page is intentionally vulnerable to content injection, including DOM-based XSS.
Visiting links to page in the browser can result in the execution of arbitrary JavaScript.
This page was intended for demonstrative and educationalpurposes only.
Interact with it at your own risk.
